Train Depot Information Instructions

Here are the simple instructions for the simple train depot page.

  • The following items are required on the input page:
  • Name of the train - input field will accept any characters.
  • Destination of the train - input field will accept characters.
  • Frequency of the train - only positive integers greater than 0 will be accepted.
  • First arrival time of the train - only times listed in military format (HH:mm) will be accepted.
